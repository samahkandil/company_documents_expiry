Company Documents

Overview

Company documents with such necessary information must be saved and used accordingly. 'Company Documents' is a useful tool that can help you to store and manage Company related documents like certificates, reports , license etc. The application also allows you to set an alert message on reaching the expiration/any other related dates of a document (like an expiration of license)
